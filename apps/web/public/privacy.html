Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Prism ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile and web applications.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you voluntarily provide to us when you register for the app. This includes:

  • Account Information: Your email address and a secure, encrypted password.
  • Usage Data: The categories, goals, and time-tracking activities you log within the application.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide, operate, and maintain the functionality of our application.
  • To securely authenticate your account across your devices.
  • To store and sync your time-tracking data so it is available to you at all times.

3. Data Security and Storage

Your data is securely stored using Supabase, which provides industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

4. Data Deletion

You have the right to request the deletion of your personal data. If you wish to delete your account and all associated time-tracking logs, please contact us at our support email, and we will securely erase your information from our databases.

5. Third-Party Services

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personally identifiable information to outside parties. Our infrastructure runs on secure third-party providers (like Vercel and Supabase) strictly for the purpose of operating the app.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, please contact us at the email provided on our Google Play Store listing.
